How to get involved:

Volunteers should be reliable and committed, caring and have a passion for helping people. In addition, all volunteers must be:

  • Committed to fulfilling at least a one year commitment.
  • Committed and available to complete a minimum of 4 hours per week, each week, for a minimum of one year
  • Up to date on all vaccinations and have completed and pass a Tspot blood test.
  • Available to receive the required seasonal flu vaccine.
  • Able to complete and pass a criminal background check.
  • Note:A program for high school and college students is held each summer for a 2 month long period. The Student program is closed for 2019.
